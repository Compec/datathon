BOĞAZİÇİ GAME JAM

COMPEC tarafından düzenlenen Boğaziçi Game Jam 4-6 Haziran'da Discord üzerinden düzenlenecek olan 48 saatlik oyun geliştirme maratonudur. Etkinlik başladığında verilecek temaya göre katılımcılar bireysel olarak veya ekipleriyle oyunlar geliştirir. Jam bitiminde sunumlar ile beraber jüri oylaması yapılacak ve ilk 3 oyuna toplam 10.000₺ ödül verilecektir.

Boğaziçi Game Jam Nedir?

COMPEC tarafından bu yıl ilk defa düzenlenen Boğaziçi Game Jam 48 saatlik oyun geliştirme maratonudur. Etkinlik başladığında açıklanacak temaya göre katılımcılar bireysel olarak veya ekipleriyle oyunlar geliştirir. Etkinlik bittiğinde sunumlar yapılacak ve sunumlar anında jüriler oyunları önceden belirtilen kriterlere göre oylayacaktır. Dereceye giren ilk 3 oyunu geliştiren takımlara toplamda 10.000₺ ödül verilecektir.

Sıkça Sorulan Sorular

  1. How do I participate?

    To compete at Hack Bogazici you must form a team of 2-4 people and fill out a registration form as a team here. Your team needs to be minimum of two people to be eligible

  2. Who can participate?

    Everyone,
    Undergraduate, Graduate, PhD Students or maximum 3-4 year work experience. If more experienced, do not hesitate to apply. As long as we have room, we will try accept all the teams

  3. What will the subject of Hack Boğaziçi?

    Creating a live and demonstrable prototype which can tackle problems about AI, Image recognition, NLP and Machine Learning

  4. Can I use whatever technology that I like?

    There is no limit on what you can use

  5. What are you expecting from us in the end?

    We are expecting to see a working prototype of your idea

  6. Can I change my team on the event day?

    No, you can't

  7. Do we need to stay in the venue during the event?

    Yes

  8. Which language will be used for presentations?

    Turkish or English

  9. How long will my presentation take?

    8 Minutes Presentation and 2 Minutes questions&answers from Jury

  10. Will every Team make presentation?

    No, only those who made prototype will make presentation.

  11. Which criteria will you use?

    Throughout the event, the jury and mentors will observe your work, your approach to the problem and your solution and regarding these criteria:
    • How the solution is related to the theme and problem
    • Creativity
    • Usefulness
    • Presentation / Demonstration

  12. What we should bring with ourselves?

    We can only provide electricity and internet connection. You should bring your own PC and relevant components

  13. Will there be food?

    Yes, we will provide food and drinks

  14. Should we apply individually?

    No, you have to apply as a team

  15. If I have any questions,?

    hello@boun.compec.org or Contact Us
